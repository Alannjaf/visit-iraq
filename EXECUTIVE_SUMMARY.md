# Visit Iraq - Executive Summary

**One-Page Investor Overview**

---

## The Opportunity

**Visit Iraq** is a comprehensive tourism directory platform connecting travelers with authentic Iraqi experiences. We're positioned to capture a rapidly growing market of 5+ million Iraqi diaspora and adventure travelers discovering Iraq's 5,000+ years of heritage.

**Market Gap**: No modern, dedicated platform exists for Iraqi tourism—creating a first-mover advantage opportunity worth $50-100M in addressable market.

---

## The Solution

A production-ready platform featuring:
- Multi-category listings (accommodations, attractions, tours, events)
- Admin-verified quality control
- Multi-language support (English, Arabic, Kurdish)
- Modern, mobile-first design
- Trust & safety features

**Status**: MVP complete, ready to launch

---

## Business Model

**Multi-Stream Revenue**:
- Premium listings: $29-99/month per host
- Commission model: 10-15% on bookings (Year 2)
- Advertising revenue: Sponsored listings (Year 2+)

**Projections**:
- Year 1: $50K revenue, -$40K net (growth phase)
- Year 2: $600K-1.2M revenue, $450K-1M net (profitable)
- Break-even: Month 10-12
